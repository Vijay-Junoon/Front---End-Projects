## About

Our mission is simple: make typing practice less of a chore and more of a game.
Whether you’re here to sharpen your typing speed, challenge your friends, or just have some fun smashing that keyboard, you’re in the right place. With random passages, a live timer, and a competitive leaderboard, TypoRush keeps you hooked while your skills skyrocket.

 - Timer
