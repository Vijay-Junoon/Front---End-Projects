About TypoRush

Welcome to TypoRush – the place where words sprint, fingers race, and typos get left in the dust! Our mission is simple: make typing practice less of a chore and more of a game.

Whether you’re here to sharpen your typing speed, challenge your friends, or just have some fun smashing that keyboard, you’re in the right place. With random passages, a live timer, and a competitive leaderboard, TypoRush keeps you hooked while your skills skyrocket.

Meet the Developer

Hi, I’m Vijay, currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering. TypoRush started out as a fun project to mix my love for coding with a playful twist on learning.

My goal with TypoRush was not just to create another typing website, but to build something that feels colorful, fun, and alive. Every animation, background, and word you type is part of an experience that makes practice feel less like work and more like a game.
